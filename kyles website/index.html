Services

At KV Solutions, we provide a variety of services for our clients. We specialize in all things indoors such as: cabinets, painting, counters, drywall, and flooring. We also specialize in outdoor needs as well such as: decking, masonry, firepits, landscaping, fencing and more. We also provide clean-out services for evicted tenants and free estimates as well.

Flooring

We remove carpet and can install any kind of flooring you need. Linolium, tile, hardwood, and Laminate flooring.

Painting

We can paint anything that can be painted. We do drywall, buildings, baseboards, and fences just to name a few.

Fencing

Your old fence needs fixed? KV Solutions can help you. We can fix or replace your fence or build a new one if youd like.

Masonry

We have a certified mason on staff who can use his skills for firepits, walls, and even walkways. We even built a retaining wall for a garden.

Decking

One thing we certainly do and do well at KV Solutions is build a deck. We can do large and small and theoretically any height to make your relaxing place the best.

Landscaping

Tree Removal, mulching, weed killing, and beautification. At KV Solutions, we know how to make your property really stand out to your neighbors.

Cabinets

At KV Solutions, we've replaced and painted cabinets for many homes and businesses including a local brewery. Let us make your kitchen really stand out.

Countertops

Just like with cabinets, we've replaced many counters in homes and businesses. We can work with about any material of your choosing for your kitchen or bar.
